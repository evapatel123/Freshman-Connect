# FreshConnect Product Requirements

## Vision

FreshConnect is a student success platform that helps incoming high school freshmen successfully transition into high school by connecting them with Link Crew leaders, mentors, classmates, clubs, events, and academic resources.

Unlike a traditional mentoring app, FreshConnect is designed to support students throughout their entire freshman year.

---

## Goals

- Help freshmen make friends.
- Connect freshmen with Link Crew leaders.
- Improve student engagement.
- Help students discover clubs.
- Provide course advice.
- Encourage participation in school events.
- Answer common freshman questions.
- Create a welcoming school community.

---

## Target Users

### Freshmen

Needs:

- Friends
- Mentors
- Advice
- Clubs
- Events

### Link Crew Leaders

Needs:

- Manage mentees
- Answer questions
- Build community

### School Administrators

Needs:

- Manage users
- Create events
- Approve mentors
- View analytics

### Platform Administrators

Needs:

- Manage schools
- Monitor platform
