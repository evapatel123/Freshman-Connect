🏫 Multi-School Platform
First screen asks "Select Your School"
Schools can request to join the platform
Each school has its own:
Students
Link Crew leaders
Administrators
Events
Discussions
School branding (logo, colors, mascot)
👤 Freshman Onboarding

Instead of just signing up, freshmen complete a 60-question personality & interests quiz.

Questions could cover:

Favorite subjects
Career interests
College goals
Sports
Clubs
Music
Gaming
Volunteering
Personality (introvert/extrovert)
Leadership
Study habits
AP/Honors interest
Whether they already have friends
Whether they're still exploring

The quiz creates an Interest Profile instead of a simple score.

🎯 Smart Matching

Instead of assigning random Link Crew leaders, use compatibility.

Example:

Freshman:

Wants engineering
Loves robotics
Plays soccer
Introverted
Interested in AP Physics

The app suggests:

⭐ 95% Match — Sarah

Robotics Captain
Future Mechanical Engineer
AP Physics
Varsity Soccer
👥 Friend Matching

Freshmen can also discover classmates with similar interests.

"You and Alex share 18 common interests."

This helps students make friends before school even starts.
